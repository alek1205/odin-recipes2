Hummus

recipe is a keeper—our family’s been making it for years!

So many readers swear it’s their FOREVER hummus recipe. If you love amazing flavor and super-smooth hummus, you’ve got to try this.

key Ingredients

  • Chickpeas are the base for hummus. The softened beans break down into a smooth paste.
  • Tahini is a paste made from ground sesame seeds, which makes hummus taste incredible.
  • Fresh lemon juice is essential for great hummus, so skip the bottled juice and buy fresh lemons—one large or two medium lemons should do.
  • Fresh garlic adds a little spice and zest. I add one small clove and find it’s just right.
  • Ground cumin and salt help to make it taste amazing, and the ground cumin adds a little more spice and richness.
  • Olive oilmakes the texture of the hummus luxurious. We also drizzle over the top of the swirly hummus when serving

How to Make Hummus from Scratch

The secret to the best hummus? It’s all about the order! Seriously, this simple trick makes a huge difference.

  1. Tahini and lemon juice go in first. Then, before you add anything else, turn on the food processor and let it run for a minute or two
  2. After a minute of being whirled, whipped, and creamed in the food processor
  3. Now, we can add the remaining hummus ingredients, but make sure you stand back and let that food processor run.
  4. I also like adding the chickpeas in two batches and processing the first batch for a minute before following up with the rest.
  5. Add a splash of cold water or aquafaba (chickpea liquid) at the very end. Pour it in while your food processor is running
